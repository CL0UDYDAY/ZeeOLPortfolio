Why you should hire me?

My name is Ann Chealzee M. Nerida, I am a graduate of Bachelor of Arts in Multimedia Arts in Lyceum of the Philippines University - Manila, and was given a Latin Honor (Cum Laude). I also graduated with honors during Senior High School in the same university, and took up the Humanities and Social Sciences Strand.

I would describe my branding as having a lively and playful vibe that permeates everything I do. I want to excite and cheer up everyone I come into contact with, especially when working with various clients, starting with my logo's friendly appearance.

In spite of my playful branding, I manage and maintain my branding by being serious about learning and listening. I also firmly think that brilliant ideas may originate from everywhere, so I'm constantly open to soaking up fresh concepts, advice, and ideas from my partners and clients. I always appreciate the chance to bring many viewpoints into the creative process, whether it be through meetings, direct criticism, or cooperative initiatives.

As we think of a cloud as being like a sponge, I am prepared to take in creativity and inspiration from wherever, and show it through my works.

I am experienced in producing publication materials, conceptualization for content creating, and problem-solving skills.

I was an associate for multimedia in LPU LIKHA from 2019 - 2020,
A creatives committee in United Leaders Associate of the Philippines from 2020 - 2021,
And also a creatives and multimedia committee in LPU Multimedia Arts Society from 2020 - 2021.

I was also the Vice President of LPU Multimedia Arts Society from 2021 - 2023, and had my internship in Madcafe Inc. in 2024.

I am also skilled in Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Premiere Pro, Canva, Capcut, and is interested in Graphic Designing, Illustrations, and Photo and Video Editing.
